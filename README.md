# SALES-DASHBOARD
This is a Sales Dashboard designed to track overall business performance in terms of revenue, cost, profit, and sales volume.

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs): These KPIs provide a quick snapshot of overall performance.
- Total Units  
- Total Cost  
- Total Revenue  
- Gross Profit
  
## Filters (Slicers): This allows users to perform detailed and dynamic analysis. 
- Country  
- Year  
- Month  

## Visualizations & Insights
### Revenue by Subcategory (Pie Chart)
Shows revenue distribution across subcategories.  
The Extra category contributes the highest share, indicating strong performance.
Profit by Subcategory (Donut Chart)
Displays profit distribution across subcategories.  
The Extra category generates the highest profit, making it both high revenue and high profit.

### Top 3 Towns (Multi-row Card)
Highlights the top-performing locations such as Berlin, Dresden, and Frankfurt based on revenue.

### Quarterly Revenue Growth (Line Chart)
Shows revenue trends across quarters.  A peak in Q3 indicates strong performance during that period.

### Monthly Gross Profit Growth (Line Chart)
Displays month-wise profit trends.  
Fluctuations indicate variations in profitability across different months.

## Business Value
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of sales performance across categories, products, locations, and time.  
It helps identify top-performing areas, understand growth trends, and supports data-driven decision-making.
