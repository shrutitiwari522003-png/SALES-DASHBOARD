# SALES-DASHBOARD
“This is a Sales Dashboard designed to track overall business performance"
## KPIs
Total units, Total cost, Total revenue and gross profit which provide a quick overview of performance.
## Slicers
filter the dashboard by country, year, and month using slicers for detailed analysis.
## Multiple Visualizations
## Pie Chart- 
This chart shows revenue by subcategory, where the ‘Extra’ category has the highest contribution, indicating it is the best-performing category.
## Donut Chart-
This chart shows profit by subcategory, where again the ‘Extra’ category generates the highest profit, making it both high revenue and high profit.
## Multi-row-card
This section shows the top three towns based on total revenue, such as Berlin, Dresden, and Frankfurt, indicating these are the highest-performing markets.
## Line Chart-
Shows quarterly revenue growth, where we can see a peak in Q3, indicating strong performance during that period.
Shows monthly gross profit growth, where fluctuations are visible, with some months showing higher profit than others.
## Overall, this dashboard provides a complete view of sales performance across categories, products, locations, and time. It helps identify top-performing areas, understand trends, and supports better decision-making.”
